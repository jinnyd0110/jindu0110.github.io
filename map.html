Mobile Gaming

Currently, with the development on the smart devices, the mobile gaming is becoming a major part of it. The history of the development of mobile games is accompanied by the iteration of mobile phone hardware. At this point, it is similar to the development of PC games. However, it's just that the development of mobile games is shorter in time, because of the different operation methods and the availability of fragmented time, the types are also more abundant. After the mobile communication entered 4G, the game also has new development. Now, mobile game is becoming a par of people’s life. The mobile game industry begins to occupy a strong marketplace. While China is competing with the United States for 5G technology, the mobile game industry will also become a new competitive target.
